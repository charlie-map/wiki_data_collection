Privacy Policy

This document contains information on how data from you (the research participants) will be used by us (the research group).

What is the software for?

This software seeks to understand trends in wikipedia usage between different people from many demographic backgrounds. We will use the data in further research for educational advancements

What demographics and data are used?

All the data utilized cannot be tracked back to any individuals. The demographics relate to:
  • Age
  • Gender / Pronouns
  • Race / Ethnicity
  • Current Level of Education
All other tracking on sites only occurs on Wikipedia where the following data is tracked:
  • Hours looking at pages
  • Votes on a page

How are demographics and data used?

All of the data we use solely for research purposes and algorithm strategies in educational software.
